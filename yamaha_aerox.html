Yamaha Mio Aerox

The Yamaha Mio Aerox is a sporty and stylish scooter designed for urban commuting and occasional leisure rides. Known for its sleek, aerodynamic design, the Aerox features sharp lines and a modern, aggressive look. It's powered by a liquid-cooled, 155cc, single-cylinder engine, which provides a smooth and powerful performance, ideal for both city roads and highways.
