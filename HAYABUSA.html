Suzuki Hayabusa

The Suzuki Hayabusa is a legendary sportbike known for its speed, design, and performance. Originally released in 1999, the Hayabusa became famous for being the fastest production motorcycle in the world at the time, with a top speed of around 194 mph (312 km/h), earning it the nickname "The Falcon" due to its aerodynamic shape.
